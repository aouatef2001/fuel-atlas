About Gasoil Tracker

Gasoil Tracker aggregates retail diesel and gasoil pump prices from government energy agencies, national statistics offices, and verified industry data providers across 40+ countries. All prices are converted to USD per litre using weekly average exchange rates and refreshed every Monday.

Historical trend data covers a 7-week rolling window. The percentage change shown on each card reflects the variation from 6 weeks ago to the current week. This tool is intended for informational and market awareness purposes only — not for commercial trading or investment decisions.
